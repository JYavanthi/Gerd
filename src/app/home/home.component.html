Welcome to GERD Registry

Gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD) is a common and chronic gastrointestinal disorder with a significant negative impact on health-related quality of life. Studies have shown prevalence in India between 7.6% to 28.5%. In this regard, GERD REGISTRY is a reliable information platform, about the patterns, trends, confounding factors in different regions of our country, which is essential towards the better management of the rising burden of GERD in India.
